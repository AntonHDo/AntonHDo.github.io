Lets Hang!

A dynamic social network tailored to rock climbing enthusiasts to facilitate community building and event planning

April 2023
Anton Do

Let's Hang! is a website that provides a platform for climbers to connect and find climbing partners. The site has a user-friendly interface that allows users to create a profile, search for other climbers, and message each other directly. Users can also search for climbers based on location and skill level, making it easier to find compatible partners. In addition to finding climbing partners, Let's Hang! offers a community section where users can post information about upcoming climbing events, share photos and videos, and ask for advice from other climbers. This feature helps to create a sense of community among climbers, and allows them to connect with others who share their passion for climbing. Overall, Let's Hang! is a valuable resource for climbers of all skill levels. The platform provides a way to find climbing partners and build a network of like-minded individuals. Whether you are a beginner looking for someone to climb with, or an experienced climber looking to expand your network, Let's Hang! has something to offer.

Petsy

A robust clone of Etsy enabling customers to browse, purchase and review unique products

March 2023
Anton Do

Petsy is an online marketplace for pet supplies where users can browse and purchase a wide variety of products for their pets. The website features a search function that allows users to filter results by category, brand, and price, as well as a shopping cart system that makes it easy to purchase multiple items at once. In addition, Petsy offers a simple and user-friendly interface that makes it easy for users to navigate the site and find what they are looking for.
